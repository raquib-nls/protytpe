About Fasal Mitra

Our Mission

Fasal Mitra is dedicated to empowering farmers across India with modern technology and traditional farming wisdom. We believe that every farmer deserves access to accurate information, expert advice, and digital tools that can transform their agricultural practices and improve their livelihoods.

Our Vision

To create a future where technology bridges the gap between traditional farming and modern agricultural science, making farming more profitable, sustainable, and accessible to everyone.

What We Offer

  • AI-powered crop disease detection and treatment recommendations
  • Real-time weather forecasts and agricultural alerts
  • Soil health analysis and fertilizer recommendations
  • Market price updates from over 2,000 mandis across India
  • Multilingual support in Hindi, English, and regional languages
  • Voice-enabled queries for low-literacy farmers
  • Expert agricultural advice through our AI chatbot

Our Commitment

Our team consists of agricultural experts, data scientists, and technology professionals who are passionate about rural development and sustainable farming. We work closely with farmers, agricultural universities, and government agencies to ensure our solutions are practical, effective, and culturally appropriate.
